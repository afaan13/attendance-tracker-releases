## ✨ Features

### 🏫 Class & Student Management
Create and manage multiple classes for different grades or subjects. Add full student profiles with name, roll number, phone number, and custom notes. Everything is organized and searchable in seconds.

### ✅ One-Tap Attendance Marking
Mark every student **Present (P)**, **Absent (A)**, **Leave (L)**, or clear it — all with a single tap. Changes are saved instantly to your device and synced to the cloud with no delays.

### ☁️ Real-Time Cloud Backup
Your data is automatically backed up to the cloud after every change. Reinstall the app, reset your phone, or switch devices — simply sign in and everything is fully restored exactly as you left it.

### 👥 Multi-Teacher School Sharing
Share your school workspace with colleagues using a unique **Invite Code**. Any teacher who joins with your code gets full access to the same classes, students, and attendance records — all kept in sync across every device in real time.

### 📅 Monthly Attendance Calendar
See the entire month's attendance for every student in a single, scrollable grid. Instantly spot patterns, track absences, and identify students who need attention — all at a glance.

### 📄 Professional PDF Reports
Generate clean, print-ready PDF attendance reports for any class. Select one or multiple months and get a full report with:
- Per-student totals — Present, Absent, Leave, and Total Days
- Day-by-day attendance grid
- Summary statistics and class overview

### 💾 ZIP Backup & Restore
Export a complete backup of all your data as a single ZIP file to save locally or share. Restore from any previous backup without losing or duplicating any existing records.

### 🎨 10+ Premium Themes
Personalize your app with 10 hand-crafted color themes. The entire interface — cards, buttons, icons, and text — instantly updates to match your style.

### 📴 Works Fully Offline
The app works completely without internet. All data lives on your device first. The cloud is a bonus — attendance tracking never stops, even with no signal.

## 📲 Installation

> ⚠️ Since this app is distributed outside the Google Play Store, you need to allow installation from unknown sources.

### Step 1 — Allow Unknown Sources
Go to: **Settings → Security → Install Unknown Apps** and allow your browser or file manager to install apps.

*(This prompt may also appear automatically when you try to install the APK.)*

### Step 2 — Download the APK
Download the latest `app-release.apk` from the **[Releases page](https://github.com/afaan13/attendance-tracker-releases/releases/tag/1.0.0)**.

### Step 3 — Install
Open the downloaded APK file and tap **Install**. The app will be ready in a few seconds.

### Step 4 — Sign In or Create an Account
- **New teacher?** Create a school workspace with your name, school name, email, and a password.
- **Returning teacher?** Sign in with your existing email and password — all your data syncs back automatically.
- **Joining a colleague's school?** Use the **Join School** tab and enter the Invite Code your colleague shares with you.

## 🔒 Privacy & Data Security

- All data is stored **locally on your device** using an encrypted SQLite database.
- Cloud backup is powered by **Google Firebase** — a secure, enterprise-grade infrastructure used by millions of apps worldwide.
- Your data is never sold, shared, or accessed by anyone other than you and the teachers you explicitly invite via your school code.
- You can delete your account and all associated cloud data at any time from the Settings screen.
